Gallery Updates

New Exhibition: Paying tribute

This exhibition is meant to pay tribute to many famous people who left an unereasable tray during their life. Visitors have the opportunity to see paintings of various famous singers, actors and dancers.

What a wonderful world

Experience some of the most beautiful places in the world through "What a wonderful world" program, a series of breathtaking artworks that transport you all over the globe. Each artwork in this collection captures the magic and serenity of natural landscapes, popular cities and underrated gems.

Abstract Harmony

Dive into a world of abstract beauty with the "Abstract Harmony" collection. These captivating artworks explore the interplay of colors, shapes, and textures, evoking a sense of harmony and intrigue. Immerse yourself in the expressive power of abstraction and discover the hidden depths within each piece.



Current Artworks Displayed

Panda

Meet one of the most beautiful species of bear through this wornderful painting. The artwork "Panda" depicts a panda bear hanging from the Moon, repesenting their sleepy nature.

Cistac

The display presents Marina's life journey, form early beginings to nowadays. "Cistac" is a crown to artist's conceputal work during the last twenty years.

Laocoon and his Sons

Delve into the world of sculpted emotions, where stone and metal come alive with raw human expression. The "Laocoon and his Sons" depicts the Trojan priest Laocoon and his sons Antiphantes and Thymbraeus being attacked by sea serpents, inviting you to connect with the artistic interpretation of the human experience.

Featured Artists

Marina Abramovic

Marina Abramovic is a serbian artist and performer. She started as a painter, but soon moved to body art, insisting on body language and synestesys, depicting pain, passion and strength.

Michael l' Angelo

Michael l' Angelo is an Italian Renaissance sculptor, painter, architect and poet, one of the most famous and influential artists in the history of European art.

Olga Pavlovets

Olga Pavlovets is a wildlife painter renowned for her ability to capture moments of beauty and transcendence. Her paintings transport viewers to ethereal realms, inviting them to contemplate the extraordinary within the ordinary.
