Current Artists

Marina Abramovic

Marina Abramovic is a serbian artist and performer. She started as a painter, but soon moved to body art, insisting on body language and synestesys, depicting pain, passion and strength.

Michael l' Angelo

Michael l' Angelo is an Italian Renaissance sculptor, painter, architect and poet, one of the most famous and influential artists in the history of European art.

Olga Pavlovets

Olga Pavlovets is a wildlife painter renowned for her ability to capture moments of beauty and transcendence. Her paintings transport viewers to ethereal realms, inviting them to contemplate the extraordinary within the ordinary.

Future Artists

Nick Davis

Nick Davis is an accomplished painter known for his vivid and expressive artworks. His use of vibrant colors and dynamic brushwork creates captivating visual experiences. Explore Nick's portfolio and discover the world through his unique artistic lens.

Past Artists

Nova "Lyric" Anderson

Nova "Lyric" Anderson is an extraordinary rapper and lyricist who commands the stage with her unparalleled energy and captivating wordplay. Hailing from the vibrant streets of the urban music scene, Lyric's powerful voice and insightful storytelling have made her a force to be reckoned with in the world of hip-hop.

Isabella Moonlight

Isabella Moonlight is a contemporary mixed-media artist known for her captivating and enigmatic creations. With a passion for exploring the interplay of light and shadow, she crafts mesmerizing artworks that transport viewers to ethereal realms. Isabella's unique artistic style combines elements of surrealism, symbolism, and abstraction, resulting in thought-provoking compositions that invite introspection.
